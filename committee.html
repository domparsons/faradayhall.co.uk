Meet Our Committee

The Committee is the student body in charge of running the hall. Each member is elected to their post by fellow students during a challenging but exciting Hustings week. In the run-up to freshers you’ll be hearing from the committee quite a lot. Every member of the committee loves to have fun whilst also ensuring that the particular aspect of the hall that they are responsible for runs efficiently. On behalf of Faraday, they would like to wish you a warm welcome to Faraday Hall!!

Nic Ponticos

Hall Chair

James Simpson

Vice Chair

Nic is the Hall Chair and it is his job to oversee each section of the committee and to make sure that it runs smoothly. Nic works closely with the warden, Dr Ash Casey, and his team of Sub Wardens to make sure that each student has the best possible Faraday experience! It is certainly no accident that Faraday is famous for it’s unrivalled student experience. Nic also acts as the figurehead for Faraday when communicating with the University and the Loughborough Student’s Union. Nic is assisted in his large number of duties by the Vice Chair, James. It is James’s job to help Nic run the hall and to support him in any way he can.
